# 📦 Supply Chain Delay Analytics

> An end-to-end Business Analytics project that analyzes delivery delays, profitability, and operational bottlenecks using Python.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Efficient supply chain management plays a critical role in customer satisfaction and business profitability. This project analyzes supply chain delivery performance to identify the key factors contributing to late deliveries and their impact on profitability.

The analysis includes data cleaning, feature engineering, KPI generation, profitability analysis, bottleneck detection, root cause analysis, and time-based delay analysis to provide actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze delivery performance across the supply chain.
- Measure business KPIs related to delivery operations.
- Identify bottlenecks causing late deliveries.
- Analyze the impact of delays on profitability.
- Discover regional and operational factors affecting delays.
- Study monthly, weekly, and hourly delay trends.
- Provide business recommendations for improving operational efficiency.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Delivery delays significantly affect operational performance.
- Some regions experience a higher percentage of delayed deliveries.
- Shipping mode and customer segment strongly influence delivery performance.
- Higher delivery delays generally reduce average profitability.
- Delay patterns vary across months, weekdays, and hours.
- Identifying bottlenecks helps improve supply chain efficiency.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Improve logistics planning in high-delay regions.
- Optimize shipping modes with high delay percentages.
- Reduce order processing time.
- Monitor delay KPIs through interactive dashboards.
- Allocate additional resources during peak periods.
- Continuously track delivery performance.
